Socialising Kittens

Ensuring kittens socialize properly is vital for their development. Start handling them gently from an early age to get them accustomed to human touch. Hold, pet, and talk to them softly to build trust and reduce fear of humans.

Introduce them to various environments and sounds gradually. Expose them to household noises like the vacuum cleaner, television, and washing machine to help them become comfortable with different sounds and settings.

Encourage playtime with a variety of toys. Interactive play with feather wands, balls, and other toys helps kittens learn to engage positively and burn off energy, reducing aggressive behavior.

Allow the kittens to interact with other animals under supervision. Gradual, controlled introductions to other pets in the household can help them learn social cues and develop positive relationships with other animals.
